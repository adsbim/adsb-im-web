Why am I seeing more than one link here?

Most users will directly be forwarded to the correct local feeder image, but in some cases we aren't able to determine the correct address. That could be because you are running multiple feeder images in your home network (cool!) or because your external IP address has recently changed, or because your ISP uses CNAT which means that multiple customers are sharing the same external IP address.

Hopefully the hostnames (or indications of a firstboot / installation attempt) will help you identify the correct link to use.

Don't worry, others cannot use these links to get to a system in your home network - the only place these links will work is when used from a system that is alreay on your home network.
